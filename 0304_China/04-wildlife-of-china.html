The giant panda is China's most famous animal! These big, fluffy bears are black and white and love to eat bamboo. Pandas spend most of their day eating because they need to eat a lot of bamboo to get enough energy. Baby pandas are called cubs and they are very tiny when they are born - about the size of a mouse! Many of China's animals need protection because there are not many of them left. People in China are working hard to save these animals by creating safe places for them to live and making laws to protect them. They also teach children about why it's important to take care of wildlife.
