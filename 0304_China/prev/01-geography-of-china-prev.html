Geography of China

Learning About China's Location and Size

Where is China?

China is a huge country in Asia. Asia is the biggest continent in the world. China is so big that it touches many other countries. It has borders with 14 different countries! How Big is China?

China is the third biggest country in the whole world! Only Russia and Canada are bigger than China. China is so big that it would take many days to drive from one side to the other side. More people live in China than in any other country in the world. Over one billion people call China their home! That's more than three times as many people as live in the United States. Why Geography Matters

China's location and size make it very important in the world. Because it is so big and has so many people, China can make many things that other countries need.
