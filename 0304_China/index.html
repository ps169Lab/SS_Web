ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

Welcome to China Web! This website contains information about the amazing country of China. You'll learn about China's geography, animals, culture, and history. China is a fascinating country with over one billion people and thousands of years of history.

TOPICS YOU CAN EXPLORE

Click on any of the links in the navigation bar to explore these interesting topics about China:

  • Geography of China - Learn about where China is located and how big it is.
  • Climate of China - Discover what the weather is like in different parts of China.
  • Landforms of China - Explore China's mountains, rivers, and deserts.
  • Wildlife of China - Meet amazing animals like giant pandas that live in China.
  • Great Places in China - Visit the Great Wall and the Forbidden City.
  • Daily Life in China - Learn how people live, work, and go to school in China.
  • Art of China - Discover beautiful Chinese paintings, pottery, and crafts.

  • History of China - Travel back in time to learn about ancient and modern China.

WHAT MAKES CHINA SPECIAL?

China is special because it has the most people of any country in the world. It has amazing inventions like paper, gunpowder, and the compass. China has beautiful art, delicious food, and incredible places like the Great Wall. Chinese people have rich traditions that go back thousands of years.
