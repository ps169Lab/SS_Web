Chinese Mythology

Amazing Stories and Magical Creatures from China

Chinese Dragons - Good and Powerful

In Chinese stories, dragons are very different from dragons in other countries. Chinese dragons are good, wise, and powerful creatures that bring good luck! They don't breathe fire or hurt people. Instead, they control the rain and rivers, helping farmers grow their crops. Chinese dragons are long and snake-like, with four legs and beautiful scales.

Chinese people believe that dragons live in the sky, in rivers, and in the ocean. The Dragon King is the most important dragon who controls all the water in the world. During Chinese New Year, people perform dragon dances with long, colorful dragon costumes to bring good luck for the new year.

The Story of Chang'e and the Moon

One of the most famous Chinese stories is about a beautiful woman named Chang'e (CHANG-uh) who lives on the moon. Long ago, there were ten suns in the sky making the Earth too hot. A brave archer named Hou Yi shot down nine suns, leaving just one. As a reward, he got a magic pill that would make him live forever.

But Chang'e took the magic pill and floated up to the moon, where she lives alone with only a rabbit for company. Chinese people look at the moon and think they can see Chang'e and her rabbit. During the Mid-Autumn Festival, families eat special moon cakes and tell this story while looking at the full moon.

The Monkey King's Adventures

Another favorite Chinese story is about the Monkey King, a magical monkey who is very strong and clever. He can fly through the clouds, change into different shapes, and fight with a magic stick that can grow as big as a mountain or as small as a needle. The Monkey King is sometimes naughty and gets into trouble, but he always tries to help good people.

The Monkey King goes on a long journey to help a monk find special books. Along the way, they meet a pig and a river monster who become their friends. Together, they have many exciting adventures and fight against evil monsters and demons.

Why These Stories Are Important

Chinese mythology teaches important lessons about being brave, kind, and smart. These stories have been told for thousands of years, and parents still tell them to their children today. The stories help explain things like why we have seasons, where rain comes from, and how people should treat each other. Chinese mythology is full of magic and wonder, but it also teaches us about what Chinese people think is important in life.
