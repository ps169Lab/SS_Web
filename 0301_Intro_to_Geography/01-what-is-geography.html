The Five Themes of Geography

Geography is organized around five big ideas that help us understand our planet: Geography has five big ideas about our planet:

  • Location: Where something is on Earth
  • Place: What a location is like, including its features and people
  • People and Places: How people change the world around them
  • Movement: How people, things, and ideas travel from place to place
  • Region: An area that has something in common, like weather or language

By learning about these ideas and tools, anyone can become great at finding their way around and learn to love our amazing world. Geography helps us become smarter about the world and how it works, which helps us solve problems like pollution, natural disasters, and disagreements between people.
