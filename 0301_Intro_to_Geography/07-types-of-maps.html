Physical Maps

Physical maps are designed to show the natural features of the Earth, such as mountains, rivers, and lakes. They are useful because they help us understand the geography of a place. When you look at a physical map, you can see where the tallest mountains are, where rivers flow, and where deserts and forests are located. Physical maps show natural things on Earth like mountains, rivers, and lakes. They help us understand what a place looks like. Political Maps

Population Maps

Population maps show how many people live in different areas. They help us understand where people live and give us an idea of how crowded or empty a place might be. Climate Maps

Climate maps show the different weather zones of the world. They are helpful because they help us know what kind of weather to expect in different parts of the world.
