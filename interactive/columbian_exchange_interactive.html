The Columbian Exchange

In 1492, Christopher Columbus sailed from Europe to the Americas. This started an enormous exchange of plants, animals, diseases, and ideas between the "Old World" (Europe, Africa, Asia) and the "New World" (the Americas). It changed everything — on both sides of the ocean. Tap any card to learn more.
