ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

Welcome to Peru Web! Peru is a country in South America. It has mountains, jungles, and a long coast. On this website, you will learn about Peru's land, special places, animals, and history.

TOPICS YOU CAN EXPLORE

Click on any of the links at the top to learn about:

  • Geography of Peru - Peru has three main parts: the Coast, the Highlands, and the Amazon Jungle.
  • Special Places in Peru - See Rainbow Mountain, the Nazca Lines, and Machu Picchu.
  • Wildlife of Peru - Learn about the animals and plants that live in Peru.
  • Peru History I - Ancient People - Find out about the Inca Empire and other old groups.
  • Peru History II - Spanish Colony - Learn how Spain took over Peru long ago.
  • Peru History III - Independence and Modern Times - See how Peru became its own country.
