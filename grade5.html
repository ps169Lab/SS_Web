GRADE 5 SOCIAL STUDIES RESOURCES

Learn about American history from prehistoric times through Reconstruction

Welcome to Grade 5 Social Studies! These units are designed to help fifth-grade students learn about American history from the earliest inhabitants through the Reconstruction era. Each unit includes interesting facts, important vocabulary words, and images to help students understand these important topics.

Unit 1: The Prehistoric Western Hemisphere

Discover what the Western Hemisphere is, learn about Pangea and continental drift, the Bering Land Bridge, and the first people to inhabit the United States.

Explore Prehistoric Times

Unit 2: Native Americans in the Western Hemisphere

Learn about the diverse Native American tribes across different regions - Arctic, Forest, Plains, Desert, and Rainforest - and how they adapted to their environments.

Explore Native Americans

Unit 3: US History

Learn about American history from the Revolution to Reconstruction, including the causes of the American Revolution, Westward Expansion, the Civil War, and more.

Explore US History

FOR TEACHERS

These Grade 5 resources focus on American history from prehistoric times through the Reconstruction era, helping students develop a comprehensive understanding of how the United States developed over time. Each unit is designed to align with elementary social studies curriculum standards and includes grade-appropriate content with vocabulary definitions and visual aids to support student learning.
