What's Special About New York City?

Cities are places where many people live and work close together. New York City is the biggest city in the United States. More than 8 million people live there! It is a very busy place with tall buildings, lots of traffic, and people from all over the world.


New York City is special because it is made up of five different parts called boroughs. Each borough is like a separate city, but they all work together as one big city. People live, work, and go to school in all five boroughs.

The Five Boroughs

The five boroughs each have their own neighborhoods and special places. All together, they make New York City one of the most interesting places in the world.

Manhattan is the most famous borough. It has very tall buildings called skyscrapers and famous places like Times Square and Central Park. This is where many people work in offices and where tourists like to visit.


Brooklyn is the biggest borough by population. Many families live there in neighborhoods with houses and apartments. Queens is known for having people from many different countries living there. The Bronx has the famous Bronx Zoo and Yankee Stadium. Staten Island is the most quiet borough with more parks and open spaces.


Famous Places to Visit

New York City has many famous landmarks that people from around the world come to see. A landmark is a special building or place that is important and well-known.


The Statue of Liberty is one of the most famous landmarks. It stands on an island in the harbor and welcomes people to America. The Empire State Building is a very tall skyscraper that lights up at night. Central Park is a huge park in the middle of Manhattan where people can play, walk, and enjoy nature.

People from Around the World

New York City is home to people from many different countries and cultures. This means you can hear many different languages spoken on the streets and eat food from all over the world.


Many people who live in New York City came from other countries to start new lives. This makes the city very diverse, which means it has many different types of people, foods, music, and traditions all in one place.

An Important City

New York City is important for business and trade. Many companies have their main offices there. The city also has important places like the United Nations, where people from different countries meet to talk about world problems.


New York City sits on islands and along rivers, which made it a good place for ships to bring goods from other places. This helped the city grow big and become one of the most important cities in the world.
