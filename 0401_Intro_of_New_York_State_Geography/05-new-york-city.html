What's Special About New York City?

Cities have many people living close together. New York City has the most people in the United States. It's important for money, culture, and business.


New York City has five big parts called boroughs. They are like slices of pizza. Each slice is different, but they make one pizza!

The Five Boroughs

Each borough is special. They make New York City amazing!

Manhattan has tall buildings and famous places like Times Square and Central Park. It's where the money is made.


Brooklyn has the most people. Queens has people from all over the world. The Bronx has the Bronx Zoo and Yankee Stadium. Staten Island has parks.


Famous Places

New York City has famous places that people know all over the world. They are important for America.


The Statue of Liberty is a green lady holding a torch. She welcomes people to America. The Empire State Building was the tallest building for a long time. Central Park is a big park in the middle of Manhattan.

Different People

New York City has people from all over the world. They speak different languages and have different traditions.


People from different countries have moved to New York City. They have created neighborhoods with their food, music, and traditions.

Money Matters

New York City is important for money. It has big banks and businesses.


New York City is on the ocean, so it's good for ships. It's like the front door to America!
