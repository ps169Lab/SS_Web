New York's Strategic Location

New York State borders the Atlantic Ocean to the southeast and two Great Lakes - Lake Erie and Lake Ontario - to the west and northwest. This unique position makes New York the only state that borders both the Great Lakes and the Atlantic Ocean, creating exceptional opportunities for trade and transportation.
