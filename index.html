ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SOCIAL STUDIES RESOURCES

Educational websites for grades 3-5 covering world history, US history, and civics

Welcome to our collection of social studies resources for elementary school students! These websites are designed to help students learn about important historical events, cultural traditions, and how our government works. Each website focuses on a specific topic and is tailored to the appropriate grade level.

Choose any of the websites below to start exploring. Each site includes interesting facts, important vocabulary words, and images to help students understand these important topics.
