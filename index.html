These resources are designed to align with elementary school social studies curriculum standards within the New York City Dept. of Education's Scope and Sequence for upper elementary. Each website includes grade-appropriate content with vocabulary definitions and visual aids to support student learning. Feel free to use these resources in your classroom as supplementary materials or for student research projects.
