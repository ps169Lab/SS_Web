The U.S. Constitution: A Simple Guide

Article What it's for
Article 1 Creates Congress (the group that writes our laws).
Article 2 Creates the Presidency (the group that enforces our laws).
Article 3 Creates the Supreme Court (the group that interprets our laws).
Article 4 Explains how states work together and with the government.
Article 5 Explains how to change (amend) the Constitution.
Article 6 States that the Constitution is the supreme law of the land.
Article 7 Explains how the Constitution was officially approved.
